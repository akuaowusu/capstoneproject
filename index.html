Welcome to Resonance: a digital environment that transports you to a new musical dimension.


Would you rather be at the beach right now listening to your favorite music? In a coffee shop? In the middle of a crowded city?
Now, you can change the ambiance of your boring bedroom with just the click of a button!

On the homepage, there are 9 background noises that you can play in different combinations, ideal for when you want to change your ambiance without listening to music. Based on your mood, you can also combine songs with the background noises of your choice. Click on any of the tabs above to find mood-based playlists, and mix-and-match the music with background noises.

Sit back, relax, and enjoy the Resonance experience!
